Hello World

Posted on Feb 7, 2022

Thanks for checking out this blog. Here’s an obligatory hello world program but in assembly :)

.intel_syntax noprefix

.section .text
.global main
main:
    lea rdi, msg
    mov rsi, 0
    mov rax, 0
    call printf
    ret

.section .data
    msg: .asciz "Hello, world!\n"

.att_syntax
